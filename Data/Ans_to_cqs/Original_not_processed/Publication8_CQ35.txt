 The model was implemented with the Detectron2 (Wu et al. 2019) library in PyTorch framework and
trained using Stochastic Gradient Descent optimiser with a learning rate of 0.0025 and momentum of
0.9. The model was trained on a single GPU (TITAN Xp) and three GPUs for different iterations. The
